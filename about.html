What is lux mundis cup?

The LuxMundi Cup is a sports event linked to the idea of "Lux Mundi," which means "Light of the World" in Latin. This phrase is often associated with Christian beliefs, representing Jesus Christ as a guiding light for his followers. For me, The LuxMundi Cup is usually organized to encourage students to do teamwork and sportsmanship among participants. For my experience, It aims to bring people together from different backgrounds to enjoy sports while celebrating their faith.
